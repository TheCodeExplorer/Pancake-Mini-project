Savory Uttapam

A thick, savory pancake from South India, made with a fermented rice and lentil batter and topped with delicious vegetables.

Ingredients

For the Batter

  • Millet grains – 1 cup
  • Rice – 2 tbsp (optional, for crispness)
  • Urad dal – 2 tbsp
  • Water – as needed (to make thick batter)
  • Salt to taste
  • ENO/baking soda – ¼ tsp

For the Toppings

  • 1 large Onion, finely chopped
  • 1 large Tomato, finely chopped
  • 1-2 Green Chilies, finely chopped
  • 1/4 cup chopped Coriander Leaves
  • 1/2 inch Ginger, finely grated
  • Oil or Ghee for cooking
